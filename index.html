Reading the body

  • This is a read-only view. Tap any day to see the plan and, once logged, what actually happened.
  • Logging happens once a day in chat, not here — the daily check-in pulls the run from Strava and asks how it felt and how the body checked. That's what fills in these cards.
  • Body check is the autoregulation signal. Fresh means go. Tight means run easy and watch it. Sore means swap for rest or spin and we talk.
  • Today is outlined in gold and moves on its own as the days pass.
